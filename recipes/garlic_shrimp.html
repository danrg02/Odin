Garlic Shrimp

Description

Garlic shrimp is a basic yet delicious dish. Eventhough it's preparation process is quite simple, the recipe varies from cook to cook, which ony means there is a whole world of possibilities when tasting this delicius meal.
This has a special place in my heart since it has been my favorite dish from my favorite chinese restaurant for almost 20 years.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Coconut oil
  • Salt
  • Lime (Optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and devein the shrimp
  2. Heat the coconut oil in a pan
  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and cook until pink
  4. Remove the shrimp from the pan
  5. Add the butter and garlic to the pan
  6. Cook until the butter is melted
  7. Add the shrimp back to the pan
  8. Cook for an additional 2 minutes
  9. Remove from the heat and add lime juice
  10. Serve and enjoy!
